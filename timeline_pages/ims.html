SeatMap Project

The largest project that I worked on during the summer was making design changes to the SeatMap platform that the IMS uses. Some background on the SeatMap platform. SeatMap, is a ticekting platform that is similar to other platforms like Ticketmaster. The IMS manages not only their own races but the INDYCAR races in Detroit and Iowa. The SeatMap platform itself was first used on the website for the Iowa INDYCAR race and the IMS planned to implement it for their own website aswell as the website for the Detroit Grand Prix. I was tasked with making some design suggesstions for the platform to make it compatible with the way tickets are sold at the IMS as well as bring it in line with contemporary ticket purchasing platforms. I made a lot of mockups in Figma and these were passed on to a developer to be implemented.
